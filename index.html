Execution States

  • WAIT — structure exists, no confirmation. No entry.
  • PENDING — valid setup, waiting trigger. Use pending orders.
  • ACTIVE — confirmation broken, trade live. Manage only.
  • MANAGE — deep in profit. No fresh entry without new structure.

Confirmation First

If a confirmation level exists on the chart, the entry is a pending order at that level. Never enter early. Near trigger ≠ trigger.

Late Entry Filter

If price has moved far from optimal entry → SKIP. Confidence downgraded heavily. Chasing not justified. (See: USDCHF Jun 12.)

Risk Model

Fixed €100 per trade. Lot size derived from SL distance per pair. Total portfolio exposure < 4.5%. Confidence >80% may receive larger allocation.

Duplicate Prevention

No automatic re-entry on an already-traded pair unless: new structure, new RR, retracement resets entry quality, or trade moved deep in profit.

Analyst Intent Override

"Stay out", "wait", "corrective", "risky" → confidence downgraded regardless of chart attractiveness. Latest PDF always overrides older PDFs.

Confidence Bands

  • 90%+ elite · 80–89% high · 70–79% decent · 60–69% caution · <60% skip

Invalidation Is Hard

Price crossing invalidation closes the trade rationale entirely. No exceptions, no re-counting hope. (See: USDCAD Jun 01 → Jun 12.)

Reading the Arrow

Use the arrow's tail (origin), not its shape. Tail near current price = move launching now (good timing). Price far along the arrow = too late.

Straight vs. bent is a drawing artifact, NOT a quality signal — hand-drawn projections. Entry is always decided by the labeled confirmation/invalidation levels, never by arrow geometry.
